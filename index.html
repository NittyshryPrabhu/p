           "Mystry of Love or Want" is a story of a boy and his beloved about how a boy feel her beloved love. In this a girl borrows a book from a boy and both of them start thinking that now they will stay together forever, which does not happen because later the boy comes to know that the girl belongs to a well-off family and after seeing her house and situation, the boy lets her go from his life. But from this story we learn that we can love anyone or you can say we can desire anyone.
